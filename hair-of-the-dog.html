- Hair of the Dog

Brand Identity Concept

Hair of the Dog is a personal project I have been working on. I dream that one day in the future I own and run my own Bloody Mary Cocktail bar, a place for people to come and relax and get their “hair of the dog” cocktail if needed.

For now the concept is but a dream, however I have been working on a potential aesthetic. I made the logo and menu in Adobe Illustrator with some extra touches added from Procreate. It has been fun to draw up some web site ideas on Figma and this is something a will definitely continue to work on.
