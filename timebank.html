- TimeBank

A Community Based App

Time bank is a community based project. Our initial idea was to create a product that can be used within a city wide radius. The app allows users to find like minded people to share activities with, whether this is an art project, participating in fitness goals or helping people that need assistance.

I worked on this project with two others over a space of two weeks, we were incredible pleased with the end product and feel that we met and exceeded the goal that we initially set out to achieve.

What we were left with is an aesthetically pleasing, easy to use app. TimeBank allows users to create accounts, check out other peoples projects and profiles, and also contact other users with the self contained chat system.
